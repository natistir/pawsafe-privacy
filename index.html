PawSafe Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy while keeping paws safe

Effective Date: July 17, 2025

Thank you for using PawSafe, the dog walking safety app that helps protect your pet's paws by checking surface temperatures. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

Location Information

  • What we collect: When you choose to use the "Current Location" feature, PawSafe accesses your device's GPS coordinates.
  • How we use it: Your location is used solely to obtain weather data for calculating surface temperatures in your area.
  • What we don't do: We do not store, save, or retain your location information on our servers or your device after the weather data is retrieved.

Weather Data Usage

  • Third-party service: We use OpenWeather API to obtain current weather conditions for your location.
  • Data transmission: Your coordinates are transmitted to OpenWeather solely to retrieve weather information relevant to your location.
  • No personal identification: Your location data is not linked to any personal identifiers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

PawSafe does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or app behavior data
  • Photos or media files
  • Contacts or social media information
  • Payment or financial information

How We Use Your Information

Location Data

  • Real-time use only: Your location is used immediately to fetch current weather conditions and is not retained.
  • Surface temperature calculation: Weather data is processed locally on your device to calculate safe walking conditions.
  • No tracking: We do not track your movements or create location histories.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

OpenWeather API

  • Weather service provider: We share your coordinates with OpenWeather to obtain weather data.
  • Limited use: OpenWeather uses your location solely to provide weather information and operates under their own privacy policy.
  • No other sharing: We do not share your information with any other third parties, advertisers, or analytics services.

No Data Sales

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

üîí Data Security

  • Local Processing: All temperature calculations and safety assessments are performed locally on your device.
  • No personal data: No personal data is transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • Secure Connections: All communications with weather services use secure HTTPS connections.

Your Privacy Rights

Location Permissions

  • Opt-in only: Location access requires your explicit permission.
  • Easy to revoke: You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.
  • Alternative options: You can use zip code input instead of GPS location.

iOS Privacy Controls

  • PawSafe respects all iOS privacy settings and permissions.
  • You maintain full control over your location data through iOS settings.

Data Retention

No Long-term Storage

  • Immediate use: Location data is used only for the duration of the weather API request.
  • No persistence: No location or personal data is stored after the app session ends.
  • Local calculations: Temperature calculations are performed in real-time and not saved.

Children's Privacy

PawSafe does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • The "Effective Date" at the top of this policy will be updated.
  • For significant changes, we will provide notice through the app or other appropriate means.
  • Continued use of PawSafe after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

International Users

PawSafe is designed for use worldwide. Weather data is obtained from international weather services, and all processing follows the same privacy principles regardless of your location.
